* Parallel questions have to do with the likelihood of self organization, enhancing the capabilities of individuals to continue self organized efforts over time, exceed the capacity of self organization to solve CPR problems without external assistance of some form
* Objectives of the chapter: defining what is meant by CPRs, how one views individual behaviours in complex and uncertain CPR situations - then examining the general problem facing individuals in CPR situations - how to organize to avoid the adverse outcomes of independent action
